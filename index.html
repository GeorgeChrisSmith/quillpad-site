About Us

Huntrix is a dynamic group from K-Pop Demon Hunters, always on the move and ready for adventure. Whether we’re taking spontaneous trips to iconic places like Summer Bay or diving into new experiences, we never know what—or who—we might encounter. On one such journey, we had the unexpected thrill of meeting Alf Stewart from Home and Away! Just like our stories, Huntrix thrives on excitement, friendship, and a touch of the supernatural. Come along as we explore worlds, face demons, and make unforgettable memories together!

Huntrix was founded in Seoul, the vibrant heart of K-Pop culture, bringing together talented demon hunters with a passion for music and adventure.
