Meet the Members

Rumi

Rumi is the fearless leader and main vocalist of HUNTR/X in K-Pop Demon Hunters, known for her powerful voice and supernatural strength. Racing star Connor Zilisch is a fan of the group, drawn to their bold style and epic storytelling.

Mira

Mira is the sharp-minded strategist of HUNTR/X in K-Pop Demon Hunters, known for her precision and cool demeanor. Despite her calm nature, she’s made it clear she’s not a fan of Alf from Home and Away—a rivalry that fans love to speculate about.

Zoey

Zoey is the energetic dancer of HUNTR/X in K-Pop Demon Hunters, known for her bold moves and vibrant personality. Just don’t confuse her with Zoey from Zoey 101 or Zoe from Sesame Street—she’s got her own spotlight and she’s not sharing it.
