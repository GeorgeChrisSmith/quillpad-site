🎭 Huntrix Meets Summer Bay

[Scene: A sunny afternoon at Summer Bay beach. The Huntrix girls arrive for a surprise visit.]

Rumi: (smiling) So this is Summer Bay? It’s peaceful… almost too peaceful.

Zoey: (looking around) I like it! But if one more person calls me “Zoey from Zoey 101,” I’m diving into the ocean.

Mira: (arms crossed) Just don’t let Alf show up. I’m not in the mood for grumpy fishermen vibes.

[Michael and Pippa walk over, curious.]

Pippa: Hello there! You must be… performers?

Rumi: We’re Huntrix. K-pop meets demon hunting. You could say we’re multitaskers.

Michael: (chuckling) That’s a new one for Summer Bay. Welcome!

[Sally and Finlay join, excited.]

Sally: Wait—are you the Huntrix? Connor Zilisch talks about you all the time!

Zoey: (grinning) We love Connor. He’s got great taste.

Finlay: So… do you fight actual demons?

Mira: (deadpan) Only when Alf’s not around.

[Everyone laughs. Damian walks up with snacks.]

Damian: You girls hungry? We’ve got lamingtons and lemonade.

Rumi: That’s the kind of demon-free energy we like.

[Scene fades out with everyone chatting and laughing by the beach.]
